Common questions

Everything you need to know

What is GigOS?

GigOS is a free gig management app for freelance musicians and performing artists. It lets you track bookings, manage advance payments, generate invoices, and monitor your income — all from your Android phone. No spreadsheets, no missed payments.

Which artists can use GigOS?

GigOS is built for any working artist who books gigs independently — DJs, singers, live bands, MCs, hosts, producers, and percussionists. If you get paid to perform and need to manage your bookings, GigOS is for you.

How do I track advance payments for gigs?

When you add a gig, you can log the agreed advance amount and mark it as requested or received. GigOS shows all pending advances in your pipeline and lets you chase them with a pre-drafted WhatsApp message in one tap.

Can I generate invoices for my gigs?

Yes — GigOS generates a professional PDF invoice directly from any gig. It includes your artist name, venue details, fee breakdown, GST support, and auto-calculated totals. You can share it instantly from the app.

Is GigOS free to download?

Yes, GigOS is free to download on Android with no subscription required. Core features including gig tracking, advance management, income dashboard, and invoice generation are all available for free.

Is GigOS available on iPhone?

GigOS is currently available on Android via Google Play. An iOS version for iPhone is in the pipeline — sign up to be notified when it launches.
